### 👋 About me

I'm a Computer Engineering student at **Sharif University of Technology**, building production front-ends with **React, Next.js, and TypeScript** — fast, type-safe, and cleanly architected. Alongside that, I work in **machine learning and computer vision**, from my bachelor's thesis on reliable Persian text-to-speech to perception systems for autonomous driving.

- 🔭 Currently shipping type-safe web apps and a multi-method Persian TTS service
- 🌱 Exploring speech synthesis, voice cloning, and 3D/stereo computer vision

### 🎓 Featured — Bachelor's Thesis

> **Persian Text-to-Speech with Voice Cloning** · Sharif University of Technology
>
> Natural, radio-quality Persian narration in a target voice — made *reliable*. I surveyed nine TTS approaches, chose **Chatterbox**, and killed its hallucinations with a **Best-of-N + Whisper-CER** selection method (generate N candidates, transcribe each, keep the lowest character-error-rate match). Then I deployed a **multi-method FastAPI service + web UI** on an RTX 3090 with reproducible CER-based evaluation.
>
> `PyTorch` · `Chatterbox` · `Whisper` · `FastAPI` · `sherpa-onnx`
